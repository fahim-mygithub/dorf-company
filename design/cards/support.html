📿 SUPPORT — the Communion substrate

Communion is the party’s shared reserve of faith and morale. Every support builds it by helping an ally — a heal, a shield, a buff banks 1 📿 — and support cards read off it. The cards build the resource; the Class Power channels it, differently per class: the Cleric runs a passive aura that auto-discharges every 5 casts (heal-all + smite-all, scaled by your cast streak), the Bard burns it slowly to sustain a party-wide song, the Druid spends a lump to shift into a front-line beast. (The Cleric’s aura reads cast types directly instead of banking Communion — it’s the always-on member of the trio.) Communion lives on the party dict, host-side, so both the meter and the power ride the snapshot.

🟣 In combat: the Class Power

Each dwarf carries its Class Power as an orb on its portrait — a Hearthstone-style hero power. Tap your own dwarf to fire it; the orb shows the gate (its cooldown, or the resource it spends). Cards build the resource, the Class Power spends it. In co-op you only ever fire your own.

The build cost — what Support adds

Support reuses the Class Power plumbing the Tank sheet introduces — a per-dwarf power + power_cd on the party dict, ticked host-side, fired by a validated tap intent. Communion is one new counter (communion on the party dict, +1 when a card that helps an ally resolves).

The Bard and Druid powers are stances (like Enrage), so they reuse that upkeep hook: the Bard’s song drains 1 Communion at end of turn and ends at 0; Wild Shape sets a shifted flag (Guard/turn, +5 Strikes, support skills locked) for 3 turns. The Cleric’s Channel is passive — the simplest of the three: a per-cast hook tags each play 🔥 (attack) or 🙏 (skill/power), bumps a same-type streak (reset on switch) and a casts counter, and adds streak to the matching charge; on every 5th cast it fires a party heal + dmg_all sized by the two charges, then clears. No tap, no cooldown.

New ops: channel_aura (the Cleric per-cast hook: tag + streak + 5-cast discharge) · bank_communion v · spend_communion [ops] (scale by Communion, exactly like the shipped spend_devotion) · buff_ally_next v (Bard Inspiration) · the two stance powers (perform / wild_shape). Reused as-is: heal_ally, party_block, party_buff, shield_ally, dmg_all, apply/vulnerable+burn, temp/retaliate, draw. Everything else is pure data.
