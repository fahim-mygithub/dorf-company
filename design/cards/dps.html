Draft direction (2026-07-15). The DPS role, same three-layer shape as the Tank sheet: the role is a shared substrate (Momentum), each class is one Class Power that spends it, and a skill tree (post-MVP) upgrades that power. The Sorcerer’s kit already ships today; Rogue and Monk are new. Numbers are unsimmed placeholders.

⚔️ DPS — the Momentum substrate

Momentum ramps your damage as the turn builds: +1 for every attack you play, reset each turn. Sequence the turn right and the last hit is the biggest. The cards build Momentum; the Class Power spends ALL of it, and the SHAPE of the spend is the class — the Sorcerer dumps it into one enormous hit, the Rogue into one execute that ignores the block of the weak, the Monk into many small blows that each re-trigger on-hit. Momentum already ships (dmg_per_momentum) and lives on the party dict, so it rides the snapshot.

🟣 In combat: the Class Power

Each dwarf carries its Class Power as an orb on its portrait — a Hearthstone-style hero power. Tap your own dwarf to fire it; the orb shows the gate (its cooldown, or the resource it spends). Cards build the resource, the Class Power spends it. In co-op you only ever fire your own.

The build cost — what DPS adds

DPS is the lightest role to add: Momentum already exists (dmg_per_momentum ships today on Momentum Strike), so the substrate is free. It reuses the same power / power_cd Class Power plumbing as Tank and Support.

All three powers are one-shot discharges — no stance, no upkeep: each reads the dwarf’s Momentum, applies its shape, resets Momentum, and sets a 3-turn cooldown. The differences are pure op composition — Surge = buff_next_attack scaled by Momentum; Assassinate = one hit + a pierce_block flag when the target is Marked/Bloodied; Flurry = a loop of N single hits, each running the normal on-hit pipeline.

New ops: surge / assassinate / flurry (all Momentum-scaled) · pierce_block (ignore block for this hit). Reused as-is: dmg, dmg_all, dmg_per_momentum, buff_next_attack, apply_status/marked, apply/vulnerable+burn, if_bloodied, if_target_marked, on_kill, gain_energy, draw. The Sorcerer’s whole kit (Mark/Channel/Finisher) is already shipped.
